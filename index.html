Hello, primary producer
This is the game


The rules are as follows:

1. Make as much money as you can by growing, harvesting and selling different crops.
2. The more money you have on your bank account, the more profitable plant you can grow and harvest, but the growing time is longer.
3. At 1500 GKT (golden kitten thaler - the currency), you can select the fields and set them to be sowed, harvested automatically, which allows you not clicking like an idiot.

Please select the time you would like to spend playing this game:

Keyboard Shortcuts

Use the arrows to navigate on the fields!
Q - Buy a new field
W - Sow seed
E - Harvest
A - Automate production
S - Stop automation

Keep your eye on the countdown timer, choose the plants wisely and enjoy.
